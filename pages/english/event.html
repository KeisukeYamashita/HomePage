Dormitory students life

　At the Takajo Dormitory, we see the dormitory as "a place to enhance one's value and sense of worth while caring for others," and aim to create a dormitory that
・Safe and comfortable dormitory
・A dormitory where you can study
・A dormitory full of love and virtue
・Energetic dormitory
・Fun dormitory
・Clean dormitory
・Disciplined dormitory

Events at Takajo Dormitory

　At the Takajo Dormitory, we value the autonomy of our residents, and the Dormitory Student Association takes the lead in various matters related to dormitory life. Throughout the year, various events are held, such as the welcome match for new residents and the farewell party, and these events are also planned and organized by the dormitory student association.
　In terms of studying, it is easier to consult with classmates and upperclassmen than with commuting students. In addition, study sessions are held in the cafeteria before exams.
