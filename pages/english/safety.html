Ensuring safety

In addition to raising awareness of disasters that may occur in our dormitory, Takajo Dormitory holds regular emergency drills in case a disaster should occur. Part of the dormitory is located in the landslide disaster zone of Shunan City, each dormitory student needs to be aware of disasters.
