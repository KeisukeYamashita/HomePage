Educationnal Support

At Takajo Dormitory, we provide a variety of support services to make it easier for our residents to study. There are bookshelves and computers in the cafeteria that all students can freely use to help them with their studies.
　The boarders' association has a head dormitory supervisor and a study advisor who help students with their studies. Study sessions are held for regular exams, and students gather in the dining hall to study. If they do not understand anything, they can immediately ask the study counselor for help, making it an easy learning environment.
