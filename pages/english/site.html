About this site

Purpose

The purpose of the website established and operated by Takajo Dormitory of Tokuyama College of Technology is to publicize the activities both inside and outside of the dormitory, and to provide information to support the lives of the dormitory students.

Production

This website was created by dormitory students as a project for the 2020~2021 "Ideas for making Takajo dormitory a more livable place to live" project.
The source code for the homepage is available on GitHub.source code
In addition, the Takajo dormitory actively supports the use of information technology by its residents.For more information, please see Utilization of Information Technology page.
